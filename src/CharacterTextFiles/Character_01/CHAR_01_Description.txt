Leuthere grew up in the shadow lands of Nevermore. His parents were kidnapped and sacrificed by the Children of
The Two Gods. Leuthere was raised by his adoptive parents who were well-known vampire hunters. Growing up, Leuthere
grew hatred towards the Children of The Two Gods. Filled with hatred, Leuthere seeks revenge for the death of his
parents.
Agile, sharp-witted, and ambitious, Leuthere is always ready to embark a new dark journey ahead.
